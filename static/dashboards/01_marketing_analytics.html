ROAS by Channel — the SQL

Query · Channel ROAS & CPA
-- Calculate ROAS and CPA per channel -- using last-click attribution model WITH channel_spend AS ( SELECT channel, SUM(spend) AS total_spend, COUNT(DISTINCT session_id) AS sessions FROM marketing.ad_spend WHERE date BETWEEN '2024-07-01' AND '2024-09-30' GROUP BY 1 ), channel_revenue AS ( SELECT o.acquisition_channel AS channel, SUM(o.order_value) AS revenue, COUNT(o.order_id) AS conversions FROM orders.fact_orders o WHERE o.order_date BETWEEN '2024-07-01' AND '2024-09-30' GROUP BY 1 ) SELECT s.channel, s.total_spend, r.revenue, ROUND(r.revenue / s.total_spend, 2) AS roas, ROUND(s.total_spend / r.conversions, 2) AS cpa FROM channel_spend s JOIN channel_revenue r USING (channel) ORDER BY roas DESC

Business Recommendations
1 · Reallocate £20K from Meta → Paid Search

Paid Search ROAS is 1.7× higher than Meta. Projected incremental revenue: £34K with minimal risk given existing quality score history.

2 · A/B Test Single-Page Checkout

Cart→Checkout drop-off is 48%. Even a 15% improvement in checkout completion = +£87K quarterly revenue at current traffic levels.

3 · Scale Email Automation

Email has lowest CPA (£4.12) and highest CVR (4.2%). A 3-email abandoned cart flow + post-purchase sequence could capture £22K in otherwise-lost revenue.

4 · Review Affiliate Programme

ROAS 2.6× is below blended target. Audit top 20 affiliates by quality — likely a small cohort driving low-LTV voucher-code customers inflating CPA.
